Description

This WebGL application depicts a scene of a chicken crossing a road. The animated chicken "flies" across the street.

Controls:
- WASD: Move camera forward, left, backward, right
- Q/E: Pan camera left/right
- Z/X: Move camera up/down
- C/V: Tilt camera up/down
- Mouse: Click and drag to look around
- Animation buttons: Make the chicken cross the road
